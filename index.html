The Bangabandhu Satellite

The Bangabandhu Satellite-1 is the first Bangladeshi geostationary communications and broadcasting satellite. It is named after the father of the nation,Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman . It was manufactured by Thales Alenia Space and launched on 12 May 2018.
